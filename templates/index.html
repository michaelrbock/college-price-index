College Price Index is our attempt to create a Consumer Price Index to reflect the college life style. Using Venmo, we calculate the cost of things like beer, pizza, textbooks over time.

If you find this info useful, please consider donating your data to our collection to increase our accuracy. We collect info annonymously and do not have the ability to charge your account.
